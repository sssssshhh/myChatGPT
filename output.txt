The company was founded by former Oracle executive Marc Benioff in February 1999, and it has experienced rapid growth since then, making its initial public offering (IPO) in 2004. As of September 2022, Salesforce was the world's largest enterprise software firm, and it ranked 491st on the 2023 edition of the Fortune 500 list, with revenues of $31,352 million. Additionally, Salesforce is the 61st largest company in the world by market cap, valued at nearly US$153 billion, and it's also a component of the Dow Jones Industrial Average.

Salesforce's platform serves as the foundation of its business model by offering subscription-based access to its cloud services, which allows for recurring revenue and long-term customer relationships. The company is a market leader in the CRM industry with a 19.8% market share in 2020. It provides a centralized location to store, track, and manage customer information, helping businesses to break down silos between departments and build strong customer relationships. Salesforce's software is designed to work for various industries and businesses of all sizes and at any stage of growth.

In March 2023, Salesforce announced Einstein GPT, their Generative AI applications built on the ChatGPT platform by OpenAI. This innovative product represents an extension of the Einstein product line, which has traditionally offered features such as automation and smart suggestions to improve business operations. However, it's important to note that some of these advanced features may require additional paid add-ons.

Salesforce's main office is located in Salesforce Tower, a 61-story skyscraper in the South of Market district of downtown San Francisco, which is the tallest building in the city and the second-tallest in California and west of the Mississippi River.

Furthermore, Salesforce has expanded its software offerings by acquiring Slack Technologies in 2020, which developed the cloud-based team communication platform Slack. Slack is used primarily by businesses and offers both free and paid subscription options.
